My Top 10 Anime

I have been watching anime for quite some time now. Though my passion for anime is not as high as some fans, I still enjoy watching anime a lot. And watching anime I have begun to like some animes more than others. This list includes my top 10 favourite animes. I didn't give them numbers as it was hard to choose 10 animes from so many and its even harder to compare these 10 and number them. And so I came up with this rough list of My Top 10 Anime.

One Piece

One Piece is one of the best animes out there and it is one of my favourites also. I watched 1 episode of One piece long ago. But back then I didn't know about this vast world of anime. By that time I had only watched fairy tail,Dragon ball,Beyblade,Pokemon and a few unpopular animes. But i liked the episode very much. But I started watching the whole thing a lot later. By the time I begun watching this I had finished a lot other animes. I was putting it aside because it was long. but I suddenly started watching it. I still watch it and I am up to date with the manga as well. I still enjoy the series and wait for new chapters each week. As I am writing this the One piece manga is at its 916th chapter.
